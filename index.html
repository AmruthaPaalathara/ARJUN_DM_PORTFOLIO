About Me

Hi, I'm Arjun D M, a dedicated Safety Manager with over 4 years of specialized experience in industrial safety governance and risk management. My expertise spans across metal industries and large-scale construction projects, where I've successfully implemented comprehensive safety programs that significantly reduced workplace incidents.

Currently serving as an Associate Manager at Hindustan Zinc Limited, I also function as the Technical Analyst to the Chief HSE & Sustainability Officer. In this dual role, I bridge the gap between technical safety implementation and strategic sustainability goals.

My approach combines rigorous safety protocols with innovative risk assessment methodologies, ensuring not just compliance but genuine workplace safety culture transformation. I'm particularly passionate about integrating safety objectives with broader ESG principles and establishing transparent reporting mechanisms that demonstrate real impact.
